Portfolio

Below are sample projects completed as part of a Full Stack Bootcamp Program.



URL Checker

Have you ever wanted to see what a website looks like prior to typing in web address into the browser? Is it safe? Is it "work-appropriate?" Now with URL Checker you can! URL Checker utilizes four APIs to display a screenshot of the website, category, warnings, IP address and reputation score. Click on the screenshot to test it out!

Technology used: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, AJAX, WhoIsXML and Thum.io APIs



Giphy

The Giphy project utilizes the Giphy API to display the top ten gifs associated with the searched term. First, search for an animal using the search box. A new button will be created and displayed at the top of the page. Next, click on the new button and the top ten gifs will display. Finally, click on a gif image to animate the gif. Finally, click on the animated gif again to stop the animation.

Technology used: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Giphy API



Train Scheduler

Train Scheduler (TS) utilizes the Google FireBase to create a simple realtime database to store train schedules. TS uses the moment.js library to calculate train times based on when the train schedule is entered.

Technology used: HTML, Bootstrap, JavaScript, FireBase



Crystal Collector

Crystal Collector is a fun and simple guessing game. The app chooses a random number (between 20-120) and randomly each crystal is given a hidden value (between 1-20). As you click on the crystals, the total points will add up. If you match the app's random number, you win; otherwise you loose.

Technology used: HTML, JavaScript



LIRI Bot

LIRI is a command line node app that takes in parameters and gives you back data. Click on the screenshot to view video.

Technology used: JavaScript, Node, APIs (Spotify, Bands in Town Artist Events and IMDB)



About Me



W. Duane Stafford

is the Chief Information Officer (CIO) for Barefoot Spas, a Richmond-based, direct to consumer hot tub and swim spa manufacturer. He is also the founder of Iconic iD, a web-based application that allow people to securely store and update emergency contact and medical information. In the event of an emergency, Iconic iD provides this information directly to certified and trained first responders. Prior to Iconic iD, Duane was the founder of PIVPointe, a Homeland Security Consulting Agency for the U.S. Department of Homeland Security, FEMA and the Governor's Office of Virginia. He has also served as the Governor's Liaison for the Latino Advisory Board.

Duane is a Richmond-native and lives in Chesterfield County. He and his wife, Dawn, have six children.
